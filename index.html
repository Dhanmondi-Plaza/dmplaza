Why Dhanmondi

A neighborhood
in Dhaka.
Reinvented in Queens.

ধানমন্ডি

Dhanmondi — from dhan (paddy, grain) and mandi (market) — is one of Dhaka's most beloved neighborhoods. Tree-lined streets, lakeside cafés, art galleries, and the kinds of conversations that shaped modern Bangladesh. It became a gathering place for intellectuals, artists, and families who built the country's cultural identity.

Naming this plaza after Dhanmondi is a deliberate act of memory — and of ambition. We're bringing its spirit to Hillside Avenue: the idea that a neighborhood can be a destination, that commerce and culture can share a building, and that a community deserves something built with care and intention.

Jamaica, Queens is our Dhanmondi.

Head Chef / Kitchen Operations Lead
Desi Dragon Full-time Queens, NY On-site
The role

We're looking for a Head Chef to build and scale the kitchen behind Desi Dragon — our Indo-Chinese restaurant concept on the cellar level of DM Plaza. We're specifically seeking cooks with a Nepali, Thai, or broader Southeast Asian background who have hands-on experience with Indo-Chinese, Chinese, or Chinese-adjacent cuisine. The flavor profiles, wok technique, and cultural familiarity are a natural fit for what we're building.

This isn't a position for someone who just wants to cook. We need someone who can build systems, lead a team, and help us scale from restaurant service into catering and events through Meridian Hill, our event space upstairs.

Responsibilities
  • Lead kitchen operations for restaurant service and event catering
  • Develop and standardize recipes, prep systems, and production schedules
  • Maintain consistency, speed, and quality during service
  • Train and supervise kitchen staff
  • Coordinate inventory usage and food cost management
  • Ensure food safety and DOH compliance at all times
  • Collaborate with ownership on menu development and operational systems
  • Scale production for events and catering through Meridian Hill
What we're looking for
  • Nepali, Thai, or Southeast Asian background with Indo-Chinese or Chinese-adjacent kitchen experience
  • Strong leadership — calm under pressure, team-first mentality
  • Organized and systems-oriented with a low ego
  • Experience in high-volume kitchen operations
  • Comfortable in both restaurant and catering/event environments
  • Able to maintain quality while building efficiency systems from the ground up
Bonus experience
  • Restaurant opening experience — you've built a kitchen from scratch before
  • Event catering and banquet production at volume
  • Inventory and food cost management systems
  • Experience training and retaining kitchen staff in a diverse team environment
Compensation $55,000–$75,000 depending on experience, with room to grow as the operation scales. Send your resume, any food photos or portfolio, and a short intro about your background and why Desi Dragon interests you.
